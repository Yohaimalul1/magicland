Privacy Policy for Fruit Frenzy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile game Fruit Frenzy (package name com.viralrush.fruitfrenzy), published by MagicLand Apps, handles information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Fruit Frenzy does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, or any other personal information, and does not track you.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves your high score locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the app.

Sharing Feature

The app includes an optional "Share score" button that uses your device's standard share menu. We do not receive or store anything you share.

Advertising and Analytics

Fruit Frenzy contains no advertisements, no analytics, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Fruit Frenzy is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
