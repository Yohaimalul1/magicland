Privacy Policy for Blaze Royale

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile game Blaze Royale (package name com.magicland.blazeroyale), published by MagicLand Apps, handles information.

Information We Collect Directly

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data ourselves. Blaze Royale does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, or any other personal information.

Data Stored on Your Device

The game saves your progress — coins, diamonds, weapons, skins, level, missions and settings — locally on your device only, using local storage. This information never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the app or reset your progress in-game.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Blaze Royale displays advertisements through Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK). To serve ads, AdMob may collect and process certain information, including your device's advertising identifier and standard technical/usage data, in accordance with Google's policies. This is used to deliver and measure ads.

You can learn how Google uses this data here: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services. You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

In-Game Currency

Coins and diamonds in Blaze Royale are virtual, earned through gameplay, and have no real-world monetary value. The game currently offers no real-money purchases.

Children's Privacy

Blaze Royale is not directed at children under 13. Because it shows ads, it is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
